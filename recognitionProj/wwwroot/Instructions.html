Instructions and Requirements

Welcome to the Recognition of Non-Jordanian Universities System

This platform is designed to facilitate the recognition process for higher education institutions outside Jordan. Below, you will find step-by-step guidance on how to navigate the system and successfully submit your application.

Instructions and Requirements for Submission of Recognition Request Form
To view the instructions for recognizing non-Jordanian higher education institutions, click here.

How to Apply

  • Complete all required sections in the system before submission.
  • Ensure you have all official documents and accreditation proofs ready before starting.
  • Follow the steps below carefully to complete your application.

Navigation Guide

The sidebar menu (on the left) contains the essential sections for your application. Each section must be fully and accurately completed before submission.

  • Institution Information: Enter university details.
  • Academic Information: Provide structured information about programs and degrees.
  • Faculties/Schools: List all faculties and schools.
  • Admission Requirements: Specify criteria for different degrees.
  • Specialization Information: Detail available specializations.
  • Hospitals for Medical Schools: Provide affiliated hospital details (if applicable).
  • Studying Duration: Outline study duration for each program.
  • Infrastructure: Describe university facilities, labs, and academic resources.
  • Library: Provide details on library facilities and research resources.
  • Official Documents: Upload accreditation documents.
  • Submit Application: Review and submit your application.

Important Notes

Keep these key points in mind before submitting your application:

  • ✅ Complete all sections before submission.
  • ✅ Any inaccurate information will be the responsibility of the institution and may expose it to legal accountability.
  • ✅ Ensure uploaded documents are clear, valid, and certified.
  • ✅ Applications with any incomplete information will not be acceptable and will not move to the recognition process.
