Non-Conventional Academic Offerings Documents

* The minimum duration of study required to obtain the degree must not be less than the minimum requirements for the equivalent degree in traditional education.

* The Non-Jordanian Higher Education Institution must offer synchronous online learning, as intended in the system for integrating e-learning in higher education institutions, for at least one-third of the total number or duration of meetings between faculty members and students, and must provide due documents to support this.

* Exams shall be held using electronic means in specific places approved by the Non-Jordanian Higher Education Institution, under its direct supervision, through mechanisms ensuring the integrity of execution, provided that due documents supporting this are submitted.

* The Non-Jordanian Higher Education Institution shall provide educational material in both synchronous and asynchronous forms, as intended in the integration system of e-learning in higher education institutions, on electronic platforms.
